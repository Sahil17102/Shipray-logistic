Understand the journey

What each shipment status really means.

Booked

The shipment record and label have been created. Pickup may still be pending.

Picked up

The courier has collected the parcel and brought it into the operating network.

In transit

The shipment is moving between hubs or travelling toward the destination city.

Out for delivery

A delivery associate has the parcel and is attempting the final doorstep delivery.

Delivered

The courier network has recorded successful handover at the destination.

Exception

An address, availability, weather or operational issue may need attention.
